7 Proven Ways to Monetize AI Websites (with Benchmarks)

1) Subscriptions

Tiered access to features (starter, pro, enterprise). Aim for >70% gross margin.

2) Pay-per-Use Credits

Let users buy credits for video renders, voice minutes, or RPG sessions.

3) Affiliates

Attach relevant products with clear CTAs. Track via UTM + network dashboard.

4) Ads

Use sparingly; better for informational blogs than conversion pages.

5) Premium Customization

Charge for custom avatars/voices/flows.

6) White-Label

One-time setup + revenue share for partners.

7) Service Bundles

Offer “48-hour launch” with guaranteed deliverables.

Benchmarks that Matter

  • CAC payback ≤ 4 months
  • ARR/employee ≥ $200k
  • NRR ≥ 110%
