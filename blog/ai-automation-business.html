AI Automation that Automates Businesses: The Blueprint

1) Validate the Niche (30 minutes)

  • Check CPC > $1.50 and low-to-medium competition keywords.
  • Look for intent words: “buy”, “pricing”, “best”, “template”.
  • Score: TAM × urgency × ability to automate.

2) Plan in Minutes, Not Weeks

Use an AI template to output executive summary, market, GTM, and a 7-day execution plan.

3) Deploy the Site

  • Static landing for speed + trust.
  • Stripe Checkout for payments.
  • Lead capture via Microsoft Forms + Power Automate.

4) Prove Revenue

Run a small paid test or post in relevant communities. First dollar in < 24h is the goal.

5) Scale What Works

Duplicate the playbook to adjacent niches. Track CAC payback, margins, and NRR.
