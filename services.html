Why Subscriptions Matter

One-time = snapshot. It shows where you stood that week. Useful as quick proof, but static.

Subscription = living proof. It evolves with your firm and keeps evidence current all year. With a subscription, you get:

  • Quarterly refreshes — MFA adoption, backups, and policies change; subscriptions keep proof aligned.
  • ROI visibility — dashboards track hours saved, fines avoided, and premium reductions.
  • Insurer acceptance — carriers expect proof that’s current, not from 11 months ago.
  • Renewal ready — your D-30 Renewal Proof Binder is automatically delivered (no midnight searches).

Think of it this way: A one-time binder proves today. A subscription binder keeps you compliant, insurer-ready, and audit-ready every day.
