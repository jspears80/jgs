How it works

Step 0 — Start with a conversation (no commitment)

We learn your firm’s size, risks, and insurer/regulator pressures, and show you what an Evidence Binder™ looks like. No contract yet—just clarity.

Step 1 — Health Check in 7 business days

Engagement begins. You receive your Essentials Binder (10 tabs): Secure Score baseline, MFA logs, backups, initial audit checklist, ROI snapshot, Scope Guard, Exit Checklist, and Executive Summary.
Outcome: fast, tangible proof.

Step 2 — Choose your binder path

  • One-Time Essentials: a bounded deliverable with no ongoing updates.
  • Subscription (Essentials / Premium / Enterprise): your binder becomes a living system with expanded artifacts and scheduled updates.

Step 3 — Build & delivery (subscription tiers)

Premium: 30 days • 40–50 tabs. Adds encryption artifacts, access reviews, incident workflows, ROI dashboards, insurer packets, regulator crosswalks.

Enterprise: 90 days • 60–70+ tabs + quarterly refresh cycles. Adds expanded packets/crosswalks, insurer acceptance letters, Binder Performance Dashboard, multi-year ROI addendum.

Step 4 — Renewal & readiness

Subscribers: Your Renewal Proof Binder is delivered D-30 before deadlines; artifacts stay current (and Enterprise refreshes quarterly).

One-Time: Binder delivered, engagement complete. You can return anytime via Step 0.
