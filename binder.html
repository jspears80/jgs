What’s inside the binder

Every Evidence Binder™ is a structured, insurer‑ready, regulator‑aligned packet of proof. Here’s what it contains.

Secure Score Evidence
  • Baseline and improvement (before/after) reports
  • Shows measurable posture change over time
Compliance Artifacts
  • MFA enforcement logs and access reviews
  • Backup confirmations and retention policies
  • Encryption settings and email transport rules
  • Incident response plans and execution logs
Audit Checklist
  • Pass/Fail/Pending controls mapped to frameworks
  • Cross‑referenced to specific artifacts
Insurer & Regulator Packets
  • Underwriting‑ready packet for carriers
  • Regulator crosswalks (e.g., HIPAA / SEC / IRS)
  • Acceptance letters where available
ROI Proof
  • ROI sheets for compliance savings, premium reductions, hours saved
  • Dashboards that visualize savings vs. cost over time
Compliance Artifact Index
  • Complete index of artifacts (ID, source, version)
  • Ensures every artifact is traceable and audit‑ready
SLA Compliance Log
  • Delivery dates and status (Pass/Fail)
  • Any service credits applied — proof of accountability
Scope Guard & Exit Checklist
  • Clear inclusions/exclusions to prevent scope creep
  • Retained vs. ceased deliverables if engagement ends
Performance & Trend Views
  • Secure Score trends and improvements
  • Year‑over‑year ROI addendums (subscriptions)
